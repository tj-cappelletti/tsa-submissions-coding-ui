Coding Submissions

Welcome to the submissions portal for PA TSA Coding competitions. Both Middle School and High School events will be able to use this portal to both view the prompts and submit their work.

Help & How-To
  • How to Submit: Click on the Problems page, select a problem, and upload your solution.
  • Need Assistance? Visit the Help page or contact your teacher/sponsor.
  • FAQs: Check the FAQ section for common questions.
Rules
  • Each student may submit only one solution per problem.
  • All code must be your own work.
  • Submissions must be made before the posted deadline.
  • Plagiarism will result in disqualification.
